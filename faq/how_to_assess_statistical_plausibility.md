# How to Assess Statistical Plausibility

Look for:
- Suspiciously uniform patterns
- Missing uncertainty metrics
- Model assumptions not described

Escalate when multiple anomalies align.


## Verification sequence
1. Verify citation-grounded claims
2. Verify methods traceability
3. Verify data plausibility
4. Report confidence and next action
