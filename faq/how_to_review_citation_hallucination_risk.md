# How to Review Citation Hallucination Risk

Prioritize references tied to central claims.

Check DOI, title, authors, and whether the cited study supports the exact statement.


## Verification sequence
1. Verify citation-grounded claims
2. Verify methods traceability
3. Verify data plausibility
4. Report confidence and next action
