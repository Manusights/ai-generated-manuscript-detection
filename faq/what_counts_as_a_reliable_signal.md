# What Counts as a Reliable Signal

A reliable signal is verifiable and linked to evidence quality, not writing style alone.

Use multiple independent signals before escalating.


## Verification sequence
1. Verify citation-grounded claims
2. Verify methods traceability
3. Verify data plausibility
4. Report confidence and next action
