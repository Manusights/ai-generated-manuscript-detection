# How to Decide When to Escalate

Escalate when at least two independent issues affect trust in core claims.

Examples: citation reliability issues plus non-traceable methods.


## Verification sequence
1. Verify citation-grounded claims
2. Verify methods traceability
3. Verify data plausibility
4. Report confidence and next action
