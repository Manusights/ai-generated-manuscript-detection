# Step by Step Review Protocol

## Step 1. Scope the risk
Identify sections with highest risk: references, methods, and key figures.

## Step 2. Verify citations
Sample references tied to central claims. Check DOI, title, and claim support.

## Step 3. Check methods traceability
Can another team replicate the procedure from text alone?

## Step 4. Check data plausibility
Look for suspicious precision, repeated patterns, and missing raw context.

## Step 5. Document findings
Record only verifiable observations. Separate facts from inference.

## Step 6. Recommend action
- Low risk: proceed with normal revision
- Medium risk: targeted clarifications requested
- High risk: editor escalation with evidence log
