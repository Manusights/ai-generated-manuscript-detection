# Citation Integrity Audit Guide

Citation reliability is one of the fastest high-value checks in manuscript integrity review.

## Audit scope
Prioritize references tied to central claims first.

## Audit method
- sample 15 to 25 critical references
- verify DOI and title consistency
- confirm cited paper supports the local statement
- categorize issues by severity

## Severity categories
- Minor: formatting or typo issues
- Moderate: metadata mismatch with possible support
- Severe: non-existent or claim-incompatible citation

## Escalation trigger
Escalate when severe or repeated moderate mismatches affect headline claims.
